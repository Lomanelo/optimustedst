Color Palette

Primary: Russian Violet – HEX #2B1F4F (headings, buttons) .

Accent: May Green – HEX #058C42 for CTAs and hover states
.

Backgrounds: Predominantly white or very light tints of primary colors; avoid busy patterns behind the logo for maximum legibility .

Typography :

Headings & Hero Text: SST Arabic Family (Bold) for Arabic headlines; Latin SST (Bold, all-caps) for English .

Body Copy: SST Arabic/Roman (Regular, 16px) for Arabic; SST (Regular, 14px) for English. Maintain generous line-height (1.6) for readability.

Hierarchy: H1 in brand purple, H2 in dark grey, body text in charcoal. Use tints of primary colors for subheadings and call-outs.
